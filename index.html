Know Our Story

What makes Saruaan oils different from regular cooking oils?

Saruaan oils are cold-pressed, chemical-free, and unprocessed, ensuring maximum nutrition and natural taste. They are sourced directly from small-scale Indian farmers, supporting ethical farming and fair wages.

Are Saruaan oils organic?

While Saruaan oils are free from chemicals and unprocessed, they focus on traditional, ethical farming practices rather than formal organic certification. The oils are handcrafted using age-old techniques to retain purity and nutrients.

How does Saruaan support farmers?

Saruaan works directly with small-scale farmers, ensuring they receive fair wages by eliminating middlemen. Every purchase helps sustain traditional oil-making methods and supports rural livelihoods.

What types of oils does Saruaan offer?

Saruaan offers a range of handcrafted oils, including Mustard Oil (for digestion and heart health), Coconut Oil (for cooking and skincare), Sesame Oil (rich in antioxidants), and Groundnut Oil (ideal for traditional Indian cooking).
